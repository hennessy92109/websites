About the practice

Sacred Path Tarot is based in Cork City and offers thoughtfully delivered tarot readings for those looking for insight around relationships, life direction, timing, or moments of uncertainty.

Led by Katherine Murphy, the practice is designed to feel calm, elegant, and reassuring — something closer to a private consultation than a performance.

The aim is not to overwhelm, but to help clients slow down, reflect, and leave with a clearer sense of what matters most.
