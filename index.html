About Sacred Path Tarot

Based in Cork City, Sacred Path Tarot offers readings for people seeking clarity around relationships, work, life direction, or a general sense of feeling stuck.

Sessions are led by Katherine Murphy and focus on creating a gentle, private setting where questions can be explored without pressure.

The intention is simple: to help clients slow down, tune in, and leave with a little more perspective than they arrived with.
